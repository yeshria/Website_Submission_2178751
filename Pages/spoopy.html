A list of explanations on design choices

  • Why pink? Pink is often seen as a sign of femininity and thus rarely associated with technology, or anything in STEM. As a human that loves the colour, I decided to use it to break convention :)
  • Why vines/greenery? Also something not often associated with technology, as progress often causes people to neglect nature.
  • Why informal language? Because it's a rough draft of the final product, and I see no use in refining language when I could be learning or designing better. So...it will be formal. Just not yet
  • Why the background change for the blogs? Because I felt like the vines on each side focuses the eye on the content in the center, thus promoting reading with more concentration.
  • Why no navigation bar for the blogs? Once again, that provides extra details that the reader doesn't need to absorb the information from the article. Also, when getting bored in the middle of an article, if the option to exit and go back to a portfolio page is right there...often users will go for it as it is within their line of vision. Out of sight, out of mind
  • Why the previous and next buttons on the blog pages? Well...people often binge media content due to the rise in popular streaming sites like Netflix. If someone is enjoying the writing, or even just the content, they might want to continue reading all previous posts, or posts that were made after the one they are currently on. There is also a button to return them to the main blog page.
  • Why wireframes font so chill? Because! Wireframes aer often sketched out. Thus....sketchy/handwriting-y font :)
